## What happened

The design of Election Commission appointments now sits at the center of a constitutional debate about whether Article 324 creates an institution independent enough to supervise elections credibly.

## Constitutional question

Does the post-2023 appointment framework for Election Commissioners preserve the independence that Article 324 requires for free and fair elections.

## Why it matters

Election credibility depends not only on polling-day administration but also on whether the institution supervising elections is insulated from partisan control at the point of appointment.

## Current status

After Anoop Baranwal and the 2023 appointments law, the live constitutional issue is whether the enacted model protects the Commission's independence or narrows the safeguard the Court had temporarily created.

## Tracking lanes

- Composition of the appointments committee
- Service conditions and removal safeguards under Article 324
- Interaction between Commission independence and executive control
- Litigation testing the adequacy of the 2023 framework
