## What happened

Recurring disputes over party switching, Speaker decisions, and the timing of floor tests keep turning legislative survival into a constitutional question.

## Constitutional question

How should courts and constitutional actors balance legislative stability, party discipline, democratic accountability, and timely floor tests?

## Why it matters

These disputes can decide who governs, how long a ministry survives, and how constitutional offices mediate legislative crisis.

## Canonical site note

The constitutional frame
The antidefection framework aims to reduce opportunistic instability, but its operation can also empower presiding officers and reshape legislative timing in politically decisive ways.
What readers should watch
Watch the sequence of events: resignation, disqualification, Speaker action, court intervention, and whether the assembly floor remains the decisive site for majority determination.
