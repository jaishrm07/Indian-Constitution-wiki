# Summary

Delimitation is a representation question before it is an election-technical question. It can shift parliamentary and state assembly power, alter Delhi's seat planning, and affect when reservation reforms become operational.

## Core constitutional question

How can seat redistribution reflect population change without producing an unstable or unfair federal settlement?

## Timeline

- delimitation remains constitutionally deferred and politically sensitive
- women's reservation is tied to a future delimitation exercise
- the practical timetable depends on census and legislative action

## Institutional actors

- Parliament
- Election Commission of India
- Union government
- State governments

## Source gaps

- any future delimitation notifications should be captured in `raw/`
- if Parliament clarifies the freeze or sequencing, the text should be added here first

## Open questions

- Will delimitation be treated as a technical exercise or as part of a larger representational bargain?
- How should population growth be reconciled with federal fairness?
