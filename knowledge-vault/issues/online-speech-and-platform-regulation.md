## What happened

Regulation of digital speech, takedown obligations, and platform accountability keeps testing the limits of free expression and the reach of state power in the online public sphere.

## Constitutional question

How far can the state regulate online speech and intermediary conduct without exceeding the restrictions permitted under Article 19?

## Why it matters

Online speech rules affect journalism, political communication, citizen participation, and the architecture of public discourse.

## Canonical site note

The constitutional frame
Speech regulation questions are not limited to censorship alone. They also involve procedural fairness, transparency, overbreadth, and whether regulatory burdens chill lawful speech.
What readers should watch
The most important legal questions are whether restrictions map onto constitutionally permitted grounds, whether they are proportionate, and whether affected persons have meaningful notice and remedy.
