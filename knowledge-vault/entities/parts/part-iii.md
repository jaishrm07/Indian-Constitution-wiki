# Summary

Part III is the rights chapter of the Constitution and the doctrinal center of modern constitutional litigation in India.

## Official role

This Part converts constitutional values into enforceable guarantees. It is where liberty, equality, speech, and remedies become judicially actionable.

## Reading path

The strongest reading path is relational:

- [[../articles/article-14|Article 14]] on equality and arbitrariness
- [[../articles/article-19|Article 19]] on civic freedoms
- [[../articles/article-21|Article 21]] on liberty and dignity
- [[../articles/article-32|Article 32]] on remedies
