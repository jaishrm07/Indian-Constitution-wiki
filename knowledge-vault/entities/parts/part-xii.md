## Summary

Part XII contains major fiscal and financial provisions, including key provisions that shape intergovernmental revenue relations.

## Official role

This Part is central to constitutional finance, distribution of tax proceeds, and the structural design of fiscal federalism.

## Canonical site note

Why this Part matters
Fiscal power is constitutional power. Part XII explains how revenue, apportionment, and financial authority are organised across the Union and the states.
How to read it
Readers should connect this Part with Article 269A, Article 279A, the GST framework, and wider debates about fiscal federalism and coordinated national markets.
