## Summary

The Court interpreted Article 239AA to clarify the constitutional relationship between Delhi's elected government and the Lieutenant Governor.

## Facts and issue

How executive power and democratic accountability should operate within the special constitutional framework of the National Capital Territory.

## Holding

Delhi's elected government has substantial constitutional significance, and the Lieutenant Governor is not meant to function as a general obstruction point to representative governance.

## Constitutional significance

The judgment is central to modern constitutional disputes about Delhi's status, federal asymmetry, and representative accountability.
