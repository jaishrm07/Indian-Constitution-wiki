## Summary

The decision became one of the most criticised constitutional judgments in India for its approach to liberty during the Emergency.

## Facts and issue

Whether courts could examine unlawful detention when fundamental rights enforcement was suspended during the Emergency.

## Holding

The majority took a restrictive view of liberty and judicial review during the Emergency.

## Constitutional significance

ADM Jabalpur now functions as a warning about constitutional failure under conditions of concentrated power.
