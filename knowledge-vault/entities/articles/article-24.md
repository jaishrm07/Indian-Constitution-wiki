## Summary

Article 24 covers prohibition of employment of children in factories, etc.

## Official text

24. Prohibition of employment of children in factories, etc.—No child below the age of fourteen years shall be employed to work in any factory or mine or engaged in any other hazardous employment. Right to Freedom of Religion
