# Summary

The Seventh Schedule organizes legislative competence across the Union List, State List, and Concurrent List. It is one of the core textual structures of Indian federalism.

## Why it matters

Many federalism disputes are not only about politics. They are about how the Constitution allocates lawmaking authority.
