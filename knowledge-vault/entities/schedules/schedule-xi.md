## Summary

The Eleventh Schedule identifies subjects that may be devolved to Panchayats and reflects the constitutional project of local self-government.

## Why it matters

This Schedule matters when decentralisation, local governance, and grassroots democracy enter constitutional discussion.

Why this Schedule matters
Local selfgovernment is a constitutional question, not only an administrative one. The Eleventh Schedule helps frame how power may move below the state level.
