## Summary

Judicial appointments sit at the center of debates about independence, transparency, executive power, and the constitutional structure of the courts.

## Scope

This topic tracks how Articles 124 and 217 generated the collegium system, why the NJAC experiment failed, and how live disputes over vacancies, transfers, and transparency still test judicial independence.
