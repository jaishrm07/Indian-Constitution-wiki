## Summary

Privacy in Indian constitutional law is tied to dignity, liberty, autonomy, and limits on state surveillance or data control.

## Scope

Privacy is a relatively modern constitutional vocabulary in India, but it now shapes debates on technology, governance, and individual autonomy.
