## Canonical site note

Why this topic matters
Electoral legitimacy depends on constitutional administration, not just periodic voting. Institutional design affects who conducts elections, how rolls are prepared, and how disputes are managed.
How to read the topic
Readers should start with Article 324 and then connect it to broader questions of electoral fairness, representation, and constitutional accountability.
