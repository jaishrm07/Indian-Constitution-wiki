## Summary

Local self-government brings decentralisation into the constitutional framework through Panchayats, municipalities, and functional devolution.

## Scope

This topic is essential for understanding how the Constitution imagines democracy beyond Parliament and state legislatures.

## Canonical site note

Why this topic matters
The Constitution does not treat democratic representation as exclusively national or statebased. Local selfgovernment expands the constitutional imagination of participation and administration.
How to read the topic
Readers should move between the SeventyThird and SeventyFourth Amendments, the Eleventh and Twelfth Schedules, and the broader idea of devolved constitutional governance.
