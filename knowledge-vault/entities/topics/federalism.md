# Summary

Federalism in India is a question of both text and practice. It involves the allocation of power, the design of safeguards, and the way institutions behave during conflict.

## Scope

This topic covers legislative competence, governor-state disputes, emergency intervention, amendment ratification questions, and structural protections against central overreach.
