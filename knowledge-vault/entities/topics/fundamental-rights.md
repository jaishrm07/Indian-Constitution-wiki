# Summary

Fundamental Rights are the most visible constitutional rights framework in India. They are best understood as a connected system rather than as isolated Articles.

## Scope

This topic covers equality, liberty, speech, movement, religion, minority protection, and remedies against state action.

The rights chapter has evolved through amendments, judicial interpretation, and shifts in constitutional doctrine around arbitrariness, due process, proportionality, privacy, and remedies.
