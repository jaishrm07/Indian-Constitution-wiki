## Summary

The Twenty-Fifth Amendment recast the property-rights landscape and sharpened the relationship between directive principles and enforceable rights.

## What changed

It replaced compensation language with the broader word 'amount' and strengthened the constitutional position of laws giving effect to certain directive principles.

## Canonical site note

Why it matters
The Twenty-Fifth Amendment shows the Constitution moving from a pure rights-centered frame to a more explicit balancing of rights, social reform, and directive principles. It remains central to the history of Article 31C.
