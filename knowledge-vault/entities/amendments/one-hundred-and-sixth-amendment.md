## Summary

The One Hundred and Sixth Amendment reserved seats for women in the Lok Sabha, state assemblies, and the Delhi assembly.

## What changed

It inserted Articles 330A, 332A, and 334A, and also amended Article 239AA to extend the reservation framework to Delhi.

## Canonical site note

Why it matters
The One Hundred and Sixth Amendment is the constitutional framework for women’s reservation in legislatures. It is a major equality-and-representation amendment and will remain important once delimitation and commencement questions activate it.
