## Summary

The Seventy-Fourth Amendment strengthened the constitutional basis for municipalities and urban local governance.

## What changed

It extended constitutional attention to urban local bodies and connected municipal governance to the larger project of decentralised democracy.

## Affected provisions

- [[../schedules/schedule-xii|Twelfth Schedule]]

## Canonical site note

Why it matters
Urban governance is constitutionally significant because democratic accountability and service delivery in cities depend on institutional design, not only administrative choice.
