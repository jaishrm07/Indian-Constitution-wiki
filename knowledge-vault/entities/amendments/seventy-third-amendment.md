## Summary

The Seventy-Third Amendment constitutionalised Panchayati Raj and strengthened the framework for rural local self-government.

## What changed

It created a constitutional architecture for Panchayats and tied decentralisation to democratic representation and local governance.

## Affected provisions

- [[../schedules/schedule-xi|Eleventh Schedule]]

## Canonical site note

Why it matters
The amendment brought local democracy into the constitutional frame instead of leaving it to fluctuating statutory preference.
