## Summary

The Thirty-Ninth Amendment tried to place the election of the Prime Minister, Speaker, President, and Vice-President beyond ordinary judicial review.

## What changed

It inserted Article 329A and related changes to insulate certain top-office election disputes from ordinary court scrutiny, becoming one of the most important Emergency-era tests of constitutional limitation.

## Canonical site note

Why it matters
The ThirtyNinth Amendment is a core Emergencyera amendment because it shows how constitutional form can be used to shield political outcomes from ordinary judicial correction. That is why it remains tied to the story of constitutional limits on amendment power.
Reading note
Read it alongside Article 329A and the basicstructure era. Its significance is not just historical; it marks the point where election law, constitutional review, and institutional selfprotection collided directly.
