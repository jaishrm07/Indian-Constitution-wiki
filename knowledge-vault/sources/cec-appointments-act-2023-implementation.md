## What this source is

Official Legislative Department annual report passage recording commencement of the 2023 Election Commission appointments law and the first appointments made under it.

## Why it matters

Use for election commission appointments framework, commencement date of the 2023 Act, March 2024 Election Commissioner appointments.

## Key claims or extracts

- election commission appointments framework
- commencement date of the 2023 Act
- March 2024 Election Commissioner appointments

## Limits and caveats

The Legislative Department annual report records that the Act was brought into force with effect from 2 January 2024 and notes the 15 March 2024 appointments made under it.
