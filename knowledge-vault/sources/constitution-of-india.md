# What this source is

This is the official India Code PDF of the Constitution of India and the primary textual source for the constitutional provisions used across the site.

## Why it matters

It is the canonical upstream source for Article, Part, and Schedule wording. Every interpretive page on the site should ultimately trace back here or to another official constitutional publication.

## Key claims or extracts

- Article text should be sourced from the official Constitution text rather than copied from secondary websites.
- Structural features like Parts and Schedules should also be anchored here.
- Amendment-sensitive pages should track which dated official edition was used.

## Limits and caveats

- India Code is authoritative, but PDF identifiers can change when refreshed editions are published.
- A newer Legislative Department compilation may sometimes provide the freshest official consolidated edition.
