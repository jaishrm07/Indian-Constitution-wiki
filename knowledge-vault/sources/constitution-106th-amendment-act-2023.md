# What this source is

The official enactment text for the 106th Amendment, which inserts the constitutional framework for women's reservation.

## Why it matters

It is the primary source for the reservation text, commencement design, and the links between representation and future delimitation.

## Key claims or extracts

- seats are reserved for women in the House of the People, state assemblies, and Delhi
- commencement is tied to a future delimitation exercise after the relevant census figures
- the constitutional design also interacts with Article 239AA

## Limits and caveats

This text gives the enacted rule, but implementation depends on future census and delimitation steps.
