# What this source is

This is the UN Population Division's `World Population Prospects 2024` online edition. For this project, the key files are the summary PDF, the demographic indicators notes file, and the demographic indicators medium-variant CSV.

## Why it matters

It is the cleanest official cross-country demographic baseline when the project needs comparable population, fertility, growth, aging, natural change, and migration series across countries.

## Key claims or extracts

- annual population levels are provided as of `1 July`
- the demographic indicators file combines population, growth rate, fertility, life expectancy, natural change, and net migration in one schema
- medium-variant projections run through `2100` and are suitable for baseline comparisons

## Limits and caveats

- this is not an Indian constitutional source; it provides demographic context, not legal authority
- annual values are modelled estimates or projections, not real-time population clocks
- any constitutional publication should still anchor representation analysis back to Articles 81, 82, 170, 330, and 332 and the delimitation framework
