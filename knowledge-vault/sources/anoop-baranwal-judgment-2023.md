## What this source is

Official Supreme Court judgment holding that the purely executive appointment process for Election Commissioners was constitutionally inadequate and directing an interim collegium-style mechanism.

## Why it matters

Use for Election Commission appointments, Article 324 institutional independence, appointment process reform.

## Key claims or extracts

- Election Commission appointments
- Article 324 institutional independence
- appointment process reform

## Limits and caveats

Five-judge Constitution Bench judgment delivered on 2 March 2023. Retrieve via eSCR portal using the citation (2023) 6 SCC 161. The Court directed an interim three-member appointment committee (PM, Leader of Opposition, CJI) pending legislation.
