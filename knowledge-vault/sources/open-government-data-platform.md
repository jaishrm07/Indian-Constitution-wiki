## What this source is

Government of India open data portal for machine-readable datasets and APIs published by public bodies.

## Why it matters

Use for open datasets, ministry-level statistics, machine-readable public data.

## Key claims or extracts

- open datasets
- ministry-level statistics
- machine-readable public data

## Limits and caveats

Use data.gov.in when an official department has published machine-readable data. It is useful for statistics and dashboards, but not for canonical constitutional text or judgment holdings.
