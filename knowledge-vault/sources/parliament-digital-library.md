# What this source is

This is the main official research archive for Constituent Assembly debates, parliamentary debates, and committee reports.

## Why it matters

It gives the project access to constitutional history, framing debates, and deep parliamentary context that ordinary bill trackers do not provide.

## Key claims or extracts

- best official source for Constituent Assembly material
- strong source for committee and historical legislative records

## Limits and caveats

- metadata can be uneven across older records
- many important materials are PDF-first
