## What this source is

Official Election Commission press note issued on 18 April 2024 ahead of Phase 1 of the 2024 general election, including the Commission's statement on the affidavit portal and KYC app.

## Why it matters

Use for candidate affidavit portal, ECI KYC app, candidate disclosure infrastructure, General Elections 2024 voter information.

## Limits and caveats

The official press note dated 18 April 2024 states that the ECI KYC app and the candidate affidavit portal provide information on candidate assets, liabilities, educational background, and criminal antecedents for voters.
