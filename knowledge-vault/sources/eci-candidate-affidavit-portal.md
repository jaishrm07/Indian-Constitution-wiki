## What this source is

Official Election Commission portal for public access to contesting candidates' affidavit disclosures.

## Why it matters

Use for candidate affidavits, criminal antecedent disclosure, candidate assets and liabilities.

## Key claims or extracts

- candidate affidavits
- criminal antecedent disclosure
- candidate assets and liabilities

## Limits and caveats

Use this portal for candidate affidavit publication. The Commission's 18 April 2024 press note identifies it as the public source for candidate assets, liabilities, educational background, and criminal antecedents.
