## What this source is

Official parliamentary motion extending the time for the Joint Committee examining the Constitution (One Hundred and Twenty-Ninth Amendment) Bill, 2024 and the Union Territories Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2024.

## Why it matters

Use for simultaneous elections bill status, joint committee reporting deadline, 129th Amendment legislative history.

## Key claims or extracts

- simultaneous elections bill status
- joint committee reporting deadline
- 129th Amendment legislative history

## Limits and caveats

Use this Lok Sabha motion to confirm that the Joint Committee's deadline was extended on 12 March 2025 until the first day of the last week of the Monsoon Session, 2025.
