## Why this Schedule matters

The anti-defection framework shapes how representative institutions function in practice. It can stabilise governments, but it also raises questions about debate, dissent, and leadership control inside legislatures.

## How to read it

This Schedule is best read as part of constitutional practice rather than abstract design. It affects coalition politics, floor tests, and disputes over control of the legislative process.
