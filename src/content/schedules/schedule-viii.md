## Why this Schedule matters

Language is a constitutional subject because it affects identity, education, public administration, and the balance between unity and diversity.

## How to read it

Read it with the cultural and educational rights chapter, official language provisions, and the constitutional idea that recognition can be both symbolic and practical.
