## Why this Schedule matters

The First Schedule is the Constitution's territorial index. It identifies the units that make up the Union and gives legal shape to the distinction between States and Union Territories.

## How to read it

Read this Schedule with Articles 1 to 4, because territorial identity, admission of new States, reorganisation, and constitutional updating all work together. It is the simplest territorial list in the Constitution, but one of the most important.
