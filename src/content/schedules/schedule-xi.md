## Why this Schedule matters

Local self-government is a constitutional question, not only an administrative one. The Eleventh Schedule helps frame how power may move below the state level.

## How to read it

Readers should see it as part of a longer constitutional attempt to institutionalise democratic participation closer to citizens.
