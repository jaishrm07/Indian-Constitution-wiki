## Why the case remains central

The judgment shaped one of the most consequential frameworks governing legislative discipline and political survival in India.

## Long-term impact

Whenever legislatures face defections, disqualification disputes, or government collapse, Kihoto Hollohan returns as the doctrinal anchor.
