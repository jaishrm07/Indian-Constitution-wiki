## Why the case remains central

The case converted a recurrent political standoff into a doctrinal question about constitutional time, federal balance, and the meaning of assent powers.

## Long-term impact

Future disputes over Governors, state bills, and Presidential reservation will now be measured against this ruling's insistence that constitutional silence is not neutral.
