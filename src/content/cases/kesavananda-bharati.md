## Why the case remains central

Almost every major debate on constitutional amendment, institutional design, or judicial review eventually returns to Kesavananda Bharati. It defines the outer boundary of amendment power.

## Long-term impact

The case made constitutional change possible but not unlimited. That compromise shaped later disputes involving federalism, democracy, judicial independence, and rights protection.
