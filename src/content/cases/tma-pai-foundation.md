## Why the case remains central

Whenever a dispute turns to minority educational institutions, admissions, autonomy, or the limits of regulation, this case is one of the core reference points.

## Long-term impact

The judgment became a central framework for balancing pluralism, institutional autonomy, and the state's role in maintaining educational standards.
