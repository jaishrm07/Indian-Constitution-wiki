## Why the case remains central

The case is one of the most cited starting points for understanding why election administration is treated as a constitutionally sensitive institutional domain.

## Long-term impact

Modern election cases still return to this judgment when they ask whether courts should intervene immediately or allow the electoral process to continue into the statutory challenge stage.
