## Why the case remains central

Unni Krishnan is the bridge between constitutional liberty and education policy. It helped turn access to education into a justiciable constitutional concern rather than a purely legislative choice.

## Long-term impact

The later constitutional amendment on education and the modern framework for school regulation still make more sense when read against this judgment.
