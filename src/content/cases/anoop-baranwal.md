## Why the case matters now

This case forced appointment design into the constitutional foreground. Once Parliament legislated, the central question became whether the enacted model answers the independence concerns the Court identified or only changes their form.

## Long-term impact

The judgment links electoral administration to broader constitutional ideas about impartial institutions, separation of powers, and safeguards against partisan control.
