## Why the case remains central

This is the modern starting point for India's judge-appointments system. It changed the constitutional meaning of consultation in a way that still structures every appointments debate.

## Long-term impact

Arguments about delay, transparency, reform, and institutional balance all operate in the shadow of the Second Judges Case.
