## Why the case remains central

This case explains why minority educational rights are not symbolic protections. They operate as real limits on how far the state may redesign the governance of protected institutions.

## Long-term impact

Later cases on minority education, admissions, aid, and regulation continue to build on the framework St. Xavier's established.
