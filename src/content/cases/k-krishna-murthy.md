## Why the case remains central

This case gives constitutional shape to reservation in local democracy. It separates representational quotas in elected local bodies from quotas in employment or education.

## Long-term impact

Any legal or policy debate about municipal and panchayat reservation now has to deal with the data-driven and equality-focused framework this case established.
