## Why the case remains central

Bommai is the key interpretive brake on a provision that historically invited partisan abuse. It converted a raw emergency power into one constrained by constitutional reason.

## Long-term impact

Modern arguments about governors, assembly majorities, and Union intervention in state politics are difficult to understand without Bommai.
