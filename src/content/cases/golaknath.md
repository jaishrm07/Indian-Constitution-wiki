## Why the case remains central

Golaknath is the decisive moment when the Court treated amendment power as a constitutional problem, not just a drafting problem.

## Doctrinal line

The case pushed the Constitution toward the later Kesavananda settlement by insisting that formal amendment could not be read as unlimited constitutional replacement.

## Long-term impact

Its immediate doctrinal force was later narrowed, but its influence survives in every serious discussion of amendment limits and rights protection.
