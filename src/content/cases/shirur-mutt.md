## Why the case remains central

Most serious arguments about religious denomination, protected religious affairs, and state regulation still pass through this case in some form.

## Long-term impact

Even where later judgments qualify or contest parts of its reasoning, Shirur Mutt continues to structure the vocabulary of religion cases in constitutional law.
