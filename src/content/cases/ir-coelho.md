## Why the case remains central

I.R. Coelho closes a loophole that could otherwise have turned the Ninth Schedule into a safe harbour for unconstitutional laws.

## Doctrinal line

The case makes the basic-structure doctrine operational in a concrete setting: textually protected laws still cannot damage the Constitution’s core identity.

## Long-term impact

Any serious study of constitutional immunities, amendment design, or rights-versus-legislative-protection now has to account for this decision.
