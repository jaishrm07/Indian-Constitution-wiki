## Why the case remains central

The judgment translated free speech principles into the online sphere and remains essential for understanding the constitutional limits of digital speech regulation.

## Long-term impact

As the state regulates online content and intermediary liability, Shreya Singhal remains the most immediate constitutional reference point.
