## The constitutional frame

The constitutional change is already on the books, but the operational question is timing. The amendment ties commencement to a future delimitation exercise after the relevant census figures are published, so the live issue is how Parliament, the Election Commission, and state institutions translate the text into seat allocation.

## What readers should watch

The important signals are whether enabling law appears, how Delhi is handled, and whether the rotation of reserved seats preserves the one-third design without creating avoidable constitutional friction.
