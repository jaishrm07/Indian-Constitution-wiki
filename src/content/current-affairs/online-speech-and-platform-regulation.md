## The constitutional frame

Speech regulation questions are not limited to censorship alone. They also involve procedural fairness, transparency, overbreadth, and whether regulatory burdens chill lawful speech.

## What readers should watch

The most important legal questions are whether restrictions map onto constitutionally permitted grounds, whether they are proportionate, and whether affected persons have meaningful notice and remedy.
