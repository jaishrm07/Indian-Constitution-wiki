## The constitutional frame

Article 200 gives the Governor a choice to assent, withhold assent, or reserve a bill. The live dispute is what that choice means when it is used slowly or strategically. The Court's 2025 ruling made inaction itself part of the constitutional problem.

## What readers should watch

The main signals are whether a future case or reference sharpens the timeline issue, and whether Article 201 remains a genuine constitutional safeguard rather than a route to indefinite delay.
