## Why this topic matters

Parliament is central to constitutional change, but its authority is not self-defining. Questions about amendment power, procedure, and structural limits shape some of the most important constitutional conflicts in Indian public law.

## How to read the topic

Start with the constitutional position of Parliament and then move to Article 368. The most important reading path is through amendment-era cases and the debate over whether constituent power can be broad without being unlimited.
