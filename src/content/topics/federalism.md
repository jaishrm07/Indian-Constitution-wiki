## Why this topic matters

Federalism is not just a question of legislative lists. It also appears in governor-state conflict, finance, emergency powers, ratification requirements, and the political incentives that shape central-state relations.

## How to read the topic

Readers should move between Articles dealing with emergency intervention, amendment procedure, and institutional accountability. Case law becomes especially important because it disciplines the use of broad Union powers.
