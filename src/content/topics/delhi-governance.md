## Why this topic matters

The National Capital Territory does not fit cleanly within the ordinary state model. That makes Delhi a recurring site of constitutional conflict over democratic accountability and institutional authority.

## How to read the topic

Article 239AA is the entry point. Readers should then track how courts interpret the relationship between the elected government, the Lieutenant Governor, and Parliament.
