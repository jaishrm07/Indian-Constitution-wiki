## Why this topic matters

Fundamental Rights are the most visible way many people encounter the Constitution. They shape debates on speech, protest, privacy, criminal procedure, welfare exclusion, censorship, and state coercion.

## How to read the topic

The rights chapter is best read relationally. Article 14 deals with arbitrariness and equality, Article 19 deals with civic freedoms, Article 21 governs liberty and dignity, and Article 32 converts these rights into enforceable remedies.
