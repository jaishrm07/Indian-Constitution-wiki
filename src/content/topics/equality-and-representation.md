## Why this topic matters

The Constitution does not treat equality as a purely abstract guarantee. It also uses representational devices, reservation, and tailored inclusion mechanisms to address structural disadvantage inside legislatures and public institutions.

## How to read the topic

Move between Articles 14 to 16 and the representation provisions in Part XVI. Then read the amendment history that added or refined reservation design, especially when current disputes concern sub-classification, legislative reservation, or the interaction between equality doctrine and democratic representation.
