## Why this topic matters

The Constitution relies on courts not only to resolve disputes but also to protect constitutional structure. Rights enforcement, writ jurisdiction, and judicial review all sit inside this institutional role.

## How to read the topic

This topic links Article 32, due process under Article 21, and the cases that define constitutional interpretation at moments of institutional strain.
