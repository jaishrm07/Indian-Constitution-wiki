## Why this topic matters

Few constitutional questions are more sensitive than when the state can detain without ordinary criminal process. This topic sits at the edge of constitutional legitimacy.

## How to read the topic

Readers should begin with Article 22, then ask whether safeguards are real, reviewable, and consistent with broader liberty doctrine under Article 21.
