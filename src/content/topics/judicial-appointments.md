## Why this topic matters

Appointment design is not just an administrative question. It shapes whether courts can remain institutionally independent while still being publicly accountable and constitutionally grounded.

## How to read the topic

Start with Articles 124 and 217, then move to the Second Judges Case and the NJAC judgment. After that, use the current-affairs desk to follow how vacancies, transfers, and executive delay keep re-testing the structure those cases built.
