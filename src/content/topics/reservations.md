## Why this topic matters

Reservation debates repeatedly test what equality means in a deeply unequal society. They also expose tensions between formal neutrality, representational justice, and constitutional structure.

## How to read the topic

This topic is best approached through the equality provisions, amendment history, and the case law that frames how affirmative action can be designed and limited.
