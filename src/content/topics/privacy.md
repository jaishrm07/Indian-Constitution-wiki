## Why this topic matters

Digital governance made privacy central to contemporary constitutional law. The concept now influences debates on data protection, surveillance architecture, welfare systems, and personal liberty.

## How to read the topic

Readers should connect privacy to Article 21, dignity, procedural fairness, and the constitutional challenge of regulating technology without normalising intrusive state power.
