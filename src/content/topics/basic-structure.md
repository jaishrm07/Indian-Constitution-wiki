## Why this topic matters

Without the doctrine, constitutional amendment could become constitutional replacement. The doctrine keeps Article 368 powerful but bounded.

## How to read the topic

The doctrine is best approached through Kesavananda Bharati and the later cases that rely on it when disputes arise over institutional design, democracy, federalism, and rights.
