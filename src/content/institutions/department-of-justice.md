## Why this institution matters

The constitutional politics of judicial appointments does not play out only in court. It also plays out in notifications, processing timelines, and the visible gap between recommendation and appointment.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page as the executive-side console for appointments and transfers, then pair it with the Supreme Court's collegium material for the court-side record.
