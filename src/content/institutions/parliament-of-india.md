## Why this institution matters

Parliament is not only where laws and constitutional amendments pass. It is also where institutional design gets debated, where committee reports surface, and where the paper trail for structural reform begins.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page when a constitutional issue depends on bill history, amendment procedure, committee recommendations, or parliamentary debates rather than on court doctrine alone.
