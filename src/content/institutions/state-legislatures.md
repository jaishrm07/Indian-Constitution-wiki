## Why this institution matters

State legislatures are where political conflict gets translated into constitutional procedure. That is why so many live constitutional disputes return to floor tests, sessions, bills, and legislative majority.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page when a controversy is really about how a state legislature is allowed to function and who may interrupt that process.
