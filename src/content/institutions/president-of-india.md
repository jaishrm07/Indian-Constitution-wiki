## Why this institution matters

The President's office is central whenever constitutional action is formalized through assent, proclamation, or ordinance. It helps readers separate legal form from political process.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page when a constitutional issue requires understanding who formally acts, what advice structure lies behind that act, and what constitutional threshold applies.
