## Why this institution matters

The Constitution creates a distinct election authority for local democracy. That matters because panchayat and municipal elections can otherwise be postponed through state-level delay, leaving local governance without elected renewal.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page when a constitutional issue turns on local election timing, State Election Commission independence, or the legal boundary between state government preparation and the SEC's constitutional scheduling role.
