## Why this institution matters

State governments are where federalism becomes concrete. Their conflicts with governors, Parliament, or the Union executive often define the real constitutional stakes of an issue.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page when a constitutional issue turns on state autonomy, executive power, or the interaction between state governance and the Union.
