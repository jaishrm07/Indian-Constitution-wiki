## Why this institution matters

The Election Commission is the primary institutional source for the operational side of constitutional democracy, especially where election calendars, procedure, and notifications matter.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page when a constitutional issue turns on election scheduling, conduct, or the administrative realities of voting and representation.
