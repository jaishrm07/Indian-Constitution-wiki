## Why this institution matters

The Supreme Court is where much of the Constitution's modern meaning is actually worked out. Rights, federalism, amendment limits, and institutional boundaries are all shaped through its judgments.

Use this institution page as the hub for constitutional litigation, landmark doctrine, and official judgment sources.
