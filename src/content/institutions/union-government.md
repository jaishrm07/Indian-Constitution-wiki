## Why this institution matters

The Union government is often the practical driver of constitutional controversy. Understanding its formal role and policy objectives helps readers understand why certain disputes arise in the first place.

## How to use this page

Use this institution page when following national reform proposals, executive regulation, or the Union side of major constitutional litigation.
