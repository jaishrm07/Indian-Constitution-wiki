## Why the term matters

This is one of the most politically charged phrases in Indian constitutional practice because it combines emergency reasoning with federal consequences.

## How to use it

When a state government faces dismissal, floor-test controversy, or claims of constitutional breakdown, President's Rule is usually the central constitutional concept.
