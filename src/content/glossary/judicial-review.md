## Why the term matters

Judicial review is one of the main reasons constitutional rights and structural limits can be enforced in practice rather than remaining symbolic.

## How to use it

Whenever a page asks whether a court can examine a law, executive order, proclamation, or amendment, judicial review is the operative concept.
