## Why the term matters

This phrase marks the transition from a narrow procedural reading of liberty to a much richer substantive rights jurisprudence.

## How to use it

Whenever readers encounter detention, surveillance, travel restrictions, or coercive state procedure, this phrase often explains the constitutional standard in play.
