## Why this Part matters

Part VI shows that Indian constitutionalism is not only national in practice. State institutions, including High Courts, carry much of the Constitution's day-to-day force.

## How to read it

This Part should be read together with federalism, writ jurisdiction, and the constitutional relationship between Union and State institutions.
