## Why this Part matters

Electoral administration is constitutionally foundational. This Part ensures that elections are governed through a constitutional, not merely statutory, framework.

## How to read it

Readers should connect this Part to institutional independence, representation, delimitation, and the constitutional role of the Election Commission.
