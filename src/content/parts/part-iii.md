## Why this Part matters

Part III turns constitutional values into enforceable guarantees. It is the doctrinal home of equality, speech, personal liberty, and remedies against unconstitutional state action.

## How to read it

This Part is strongest when read relationally. Article 14 controls arbitrariness, Article 19 structures civic freedom, Article 21 protects liberty and dignity, and Article 32 provides remedies.
