## Why this Part matters

Amendment power decides how stable the constitutional order is and how change can legitimately happen. In India, that question is inseparable from the basic structure doctrine.

## How to read it

Part XX should be read together with landmark amendment cases and the history of amendments that attempted deep institutional redesign.
