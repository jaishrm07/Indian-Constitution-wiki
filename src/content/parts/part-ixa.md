## Why this Part matters

Part IXA recognises that urban governance requires institutional design, representation, and devolution within the constitutional order. It is a major reason municipal structure can be debated in constitutional terms.

## How to read it

Readers should connect this Part with Article 243W, the Twelfth Schedule, and the Seventy-Fourth Amendment. That cluster explains how urban self-government is built into the constitutional map.
