  the President shall declare either that he assents to the Bill or that he
  withholds assent therefrom. Where the Bill is not a Money Bill, the President

## Why it matters

Article 201 shows how a State Bill can migrate into a Union constitutional process. It therefore matters whenever reservation is used in politically contested state legislation.

## How courts and institutions use it

The provision is important not only for formal assent mechanics, but also for understanding how reservation affects federal balance and the pace of democratic lawmaking.
