  continue for five years from the date appointed for its first meeting and no
  longer. The period may, while a Proclamation of Emergency is in operation,

## Why it matters

Article 172 is one of the core structural provisions behind debates over synchronized elections, premature dissolution, and the democratic cost of destabilizing an Assembly.

## How courts and institutions use it

The provision matters when constitutional design questions turn on how long Assemblies may continue, when they end, and how election timing interacts with federal politics.
