## Why it matters

Article 16 sits at the centre of some of the most difficult equality debates in India because it combines anti-discrimination with the constitutional accommodation of reservation policy.

## How courts use it

Courts often read Article 16 alongside Article 14 when assessing recruitment rules, promotional reservation, representational justice, and classification in public employment.
