## Why it matters

Article 13 connects the rights chapter to judicial review. It ensures that Part III is not merely declaratory and that rights have supremacy against inconsistent law.

## How courts use it

The Article becomes especially important when a challenge concerns legislative validity, continuity of pre-Constitution law, or the relationship between constitutional amendments and rights doctrine.
