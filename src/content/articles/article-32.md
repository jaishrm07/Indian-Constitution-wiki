## Why it matters

Rights without remedies can become symbolic. Article 32 gives institutional force to Part III by allowing direct access to the Supreme Court for enforcement.

## Reading the provision

The Article empowers the Court to issue writs and has long been described as part of the Constitution's remedial heart. It also reflects the centrality of judicial review in the constitutional design.

## How courts use it

Public interest litigation, urgent liberty claims, and structural rights litigation often rely on Article 32, though the Court still exercises procedural discipline over when it will intervene directly.
