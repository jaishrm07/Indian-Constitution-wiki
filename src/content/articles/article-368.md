## Why it matters

Article 368 defines how constitutional change happens. It also became the focal point for the basic structure doctrine, which prevents formal amendment power from becoming a licence for constitutional replacement.

## Reading the provision

The Article describes procedures for amendment and, in some cases, ratification by states. The politics of amendment therefore tell a story not only about parliamentary numbers but also about constitutional design.

## How courts use it

Debates around judicial review of amendments, institutional balance, and constitutional identity all converge on Article 368.
