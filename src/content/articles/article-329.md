## Why it matters

Article 329 is one of the Constitution's clearest attempts to protect an institutional process from constant interruption. It shapes when courts step in and when they wait.

## How courts use it

Courts use Article 329 to distinguish between pre-election intervention and post-election challenge, and to preserve the integrity and continuity of the electoral process.
