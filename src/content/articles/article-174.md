## Why it matters

Article 174 becomes central when disputes arise over assembly sessions, floor tests, government formation, and whether a Governor may shape legislative timing for constitutional rather than political reasons.

## How courts use it

Courts read Article 174 together with responsible government. The key question is not whether the text gives a formal power, but how that power must operate inside the wider constitutional scheme.
