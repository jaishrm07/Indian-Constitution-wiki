accessNotes: The Constituent Assembly met from 9 December 1946 to 26 November 1949 over 11 sessions. The full debates — comprising 12 volumes — are digitised and available through the Parliament Digital Library at eparlib.sansad.in as well as through the parliamentofindia.nic.in portal. They are the primary source for understanding the intent behind constitutional provisions. Searches by article number, speaker name, or keyword are supported on the Parliament Digital Library portal. The debates are frequently cited by the Supreme Court in constitutional interpretation, including landmark judgments such as Kesavananda Bharati (1973), Maneka Gandhi (1978), and Indra Sawhney (1992).
