## Why it matters

The Thirty-Ninth Amendment is a core Emergency-era amendment because it shows how constitutional form can be used to shield political outcomes from ordinary judicial correction. That is why it remains tied to the story of constitutional limits on amendment power.

## Reading note

Read it alongside Article 329A and the basic-structure era. Its significance is not just historical; it marks the point where election law, constitutional review, and institutional self-protection collided directly.
