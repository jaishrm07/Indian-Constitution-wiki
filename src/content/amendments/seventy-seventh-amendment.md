## Why it matters

The Seventy-Seventh Amendment shows that equality in public employment is not only about entry into office. It also shapes advancement, hierarchy, and how the state measures substantive representation over time.

## Reading note

Read this amendment with Article 16 and the broader reservations topic. It is one of the clearest examples of the Constitution explicitly responding to a doctrinal gap in service jurisprudence.
