## Why it matters

The Ninety-Ninth Amendment is the modern judiciary amendment that every constitutional reader has to understand. It became central to the larger question of whether Parliament can redesign judicial appointments without undermining judicial independence.

## Reading note

Read it with the judicial appointments articles and the basic-structure topic. The amendment is not just about staffing the courts; it is about who gets to shape the constitutional conditions of adjudication.
